MasterMind Help.

            

The game has selected 4 colors
from 6 possible:
  B = Blue
  W = White
  G = Green
  P = Purple
  R = Red
  Y = Yellow

Game may select the same color
any number of times.

You set colors in the boxes by
clicking on one of the six
colors. Then click on an
Answer box to put that color.

You may click on any Answer box
with current color selected.

You ask the game to
check your four colors by
clicking the Check Answer button.

The game displays your guess
and provides you clues.

The clues mean:
  X = Right color, right position.
  O = Right color, bad position.

Any 'X' shown could be any color.
IE, if Guess shows B W G Y and
clues are 'XX', could be either:
B and W, B and G, B and Y,
W and G, W and Y, OR G and Y
are the right colors in the right
position.

You have 10 chances to win.

You restart a game by clicking
on the Restart Game button.
